Fritz-Walter Stadium

Fritz-Walter Stadium, formerly known as the Betzenburgstadion, is the home stadium of 1. FC Kaiserslautern. With a capacity of 49,700, it its the 13th largest stadium in Germany, and has a higher capacity than other stadiums from other, larger cities around Germany. Both Hannover and Leipzig are cities larger than Kaiserslautern, yet have smaller soccer stadiums, with capacities of 49,000 and 42,960, respectively.

Like many other German stadiums, Fritz-Walter Stadium was expanded in 2006 in order to accomadate for the increased attendance for spectators around the world. The most important match that took place was future champions Italy's match agains Australia, where they won 1-0.
